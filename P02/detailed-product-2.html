GMK Modern Dolch 2 Keycap Set

$99.99

In 1989 Dolch Computer Systems unveiled the Dolch PAC 486, and unknowingly created a colourway that would be loved by keyboard-enthusiasts for years to come. From 1:1 remakes to more playful iterations like Mint Dolch, very few sets have reached such popularity as this one. We decided it was time to truly bring Dolch to modern times. MODERN DOLCH 2 keeps the gorgeous light-grey and dark-grey contrast of the original, but with cooler and more modern colors while adding some striking accents to truly bring the set alive.

Product Info:

  • Cherry profile
  • 1.5mm thick doubleshot ABS plastic
  • Manufactured by GMK
  • Made in the United States

About GMK:

GMK electronic design is an well-established, medium-sized company with high efficiency.The hierarchies are flat and the short communication channels contribute to a good working atmosphere.
