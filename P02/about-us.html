About Us

ZENKEEBS is a Canadian based company and was founded in 2010 by Bob and John during their wonderous years in Simon Fraser University. They came together to share their passion for mechanical keyboards and created a place for any keyboard lover to further their hobbies. Fast forward to 2020, ZenKeebs has grown drastically from their home office to an office space and warehouse allowing them to take way more orders than before.

Meet the Team

Hi guys! I'm Bob and I like keyboards and gaming. You can usually find me behind a computer screen, managing the website when I'm not hitting the mid-lane in League of Legends.

Welcome everyone! My name is John and I'm the other half of the ZenKeebs brain. When I'm not managing ZenKeebs things, I'm usually cooking in my free time or playing with my dog.
